Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bharat Nourish flour preservative free?

Yes. Our flour is 100% preservative free and freshly milled to maintain purity and nutrition.

What makes your flour different from regular market flour?

We use traditional stone grinding (chakki method) which retains fiber, minerals, and natural taste unlike high-speed commercial roller mills.

Is it suitable for children and elderly?

Yes. Our flour is light, easy to digest, and free from additives, making it suitable for all age groups.

How should I store the flour?

Store in an airtight container in a cool and dry place. For longer freshness, refrigeration is recommended.
